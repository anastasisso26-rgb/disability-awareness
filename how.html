How YOU Can Help!

Even small actions can make a big difference in creating a more inclusive world:

  • Be Friendly and Respectful: Treat everyone with kindness. Say hello and be open to getting to know people.
  • Listen and Learn: If someone shares their experience with you, listen carefully. Don't assume you know what they need or how they feel.
  • Use Inclusive Language: Say "person with a disability" instead of "disabled person." Focus on the person, not just their disability.
  • Offer Help (Don't Force It): If you think someone might need help, ask them first: "Can I help you with something?" Respect their answer if they say no.
  • Speak Up: If you hear someone making fun of or being unkind to a person with a disability, speak up or tell a trusted adult.
  • Support Accessibility: If you see things that are not accessible (like a ramp blocked by boxes), politely point it out to an adult who can help fix it.

Your actions can inspire others and make your school and community a better place for everyone!
