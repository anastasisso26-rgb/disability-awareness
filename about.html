What is Disability?

A disability simply means that a person might do things differently or need some extra help with certain activities. It could be about how they move, see, hear, learn, or how their brain processes information.

It's important to remember that having a disability doesn't mean someone is "less than" anyone else. Everyone has strengths and challenges! A disability is just one part of who a person is, not the whole story.

The goal is to create a society where everyone can participate fully and feel respected. This is called **inclusion**.
