Inclusion & Accessibility

Inclusion means making sure everyone feels welcome and can participate fully, no matter their abilities. It's about celebrating differences and valuing every person's contribution.

Accessibility is about making sure places, information, and tools can be used by everyone. Think about ramps for wheelchairs, subtitles on videos for deaf people, or clear signs for people with low vision.

When things are accessible, everyone benefits! It makes our communities stronger and more fair for all.
