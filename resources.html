Resources and More Information

Want to learn more or find support? Here are some great places to start:

  • World Health Organization (WHO) Disability: Learn about global initiatives and information.
  • CDC Disability and Health: Information from the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention.
  • United Nations - Disability: Explore international efforts for disability rights.
  • Local Disability Advocacy Groups: Search online for organizations in your area that support people with disabilities. They often have events and educational materials!

Always check that your sources are reliable and up-to-date!
